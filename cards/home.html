Design Art About Connect

Informative Card Design

2016 | Typography I | University of Maryland, Baltimore County
I learned the fundamentals of pairing typefaces and applying them in Design, as well as how to use style sheets and other features of Adobe Indesign CC. The assignment was to choose a topic and create an A through Z deck of cards (front & back) that would serve to educate the reader.
