Design Art About Connect

Environmental Expo/Conference Fliers & Poster

2017 | Environmental Design | University of Maryland, Baltimore County
This poster and flier design were conceived for the 2018 Environmental Expo/Conference. The main clientele for this event are architects, engineers, scholars, and tech-minded persons. My design aimed to capture the sophistication of the client as well as the fun, playful nature of the environment that we live. I sought to combine elements of the city and nature to create a fusion that spoke to the potential of environmentally-conscious building projects.
