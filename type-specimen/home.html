Akzidenz Grotesque Type Specimen

2018 | Typography III | University of Maryland, Baltimore County
I designed a set of 10 original glyphs based on a close inspection of the formal qualities of classic typeface, Akzidenz Grotesque. I also typeset and designed a type specimen for the Akzidenz Grotesque type family, including the original glyphs that I created.
